                and is a warm blooded
                vertebrate (with a spine).

                • A mollusk is an invertebrate
                  (no spine) animal that lives in water
                  or damp areas and usually has
                  an external shell.

                  • A reptile is a cold blooded
                    vertebrate with dry scaly skin.

                      that eats only plants.
